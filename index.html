What is a profiled data value?

A profiled data value is a specific piece of information that is deliberately chosen and highlighted for closer investigation. This happens while studying records in a spreadsheet or database, or while reviewing information from sources such as an article, a section of a report, a chapter of a book, website content, a telephone conversation, or even a direct interaction with a person, such as a client.
