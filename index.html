ðŸ“Œ Central Entity

Central Entity is derived from the title of the application. For example, if the application is Activity Tracker, then the Central Entity is Activity. If it's Expenses Tracker, then the Central Entity is Expenses.

ðŸ“Œ Strong Synonym

We identify the strongest synonym for the Central Entity. For instance, the strongest synonym for Activity is Task, and for Expenses it is Expenditures. This synonym becomes the first Input Channel or Data Divisionâ€”commonly referred to as a field.

ðŸ“Œ Organisational Terms

These are universal terms that strengthen the input structure. If Task is the first field, organisational terms like Date and Time help structure the input. The full list includes:

  • Date
  • Time
  • Location
  • Session Activities / Session Expenses (based on Central Entity)
  • Status

Note: The fourth term dynamically reflects the plural of the Central Entity. For Activity, it's Session Activities. For Expenses, it's Session Expenses. This reflects the reality that multiple activities or expenses may occur during a single session.
